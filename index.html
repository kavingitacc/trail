About Us

The department was established in 1994. The course offered by the department is B.Tech – Chemical Engineering which is affiliated to Anna University Chennai. It is a full-time course with 8 semesters. The current annual intake is 60 students. Mode of admission is Institutional and Central (SWS). The Department has been accredited by the National Board of Accreditation and its validity is till 2022.
